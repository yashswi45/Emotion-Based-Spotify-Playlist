About

MoodSync is an AI-powered music recommendation app that creates personalized playlists based on your emotions. By analyzing your facial expressions, text sentiment, or voice tone, MoodSync detects your current mood and seamlessly integrates with Spotify to curate a playlist that matches your feelings. Whether you're feeling happy, sad, relaxed, or energetic, the right music is just a click away. Our goal is to enhance your listening experience by combining emotion recognition with intelligent music selection, ensuring that every song resonates with your mood.
